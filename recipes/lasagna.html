Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a type of pasta casserole dish. It is made with layers of pasta, sauce, and cheese. It is a popular dish in Italian cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 scoop Albahaca
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  3. Meanwhile, brown ground beef and Italian sausage in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Stir in Albahaca, Parmesan cheese, and salt and pepper to taste.
